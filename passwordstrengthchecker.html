    Why Password Strength Matters

    Strong passwords are important for protecting your accounts from hackers. Weak passwords can be easily guessed or cracked by attackers using methods such as brute force attacks and credential stuffing.

    Good Security Practices

    Following good security practices will reduce the risk of your accounts being compromised.

    • Use passwords that are at least 8 characters long with a mix of letters, numbers, symbols, uppercase and lowercase letters.
    • Do not reuse the same password across multiple accounts.
    • Use two-factor authentication (2FA).
    • Use a password manager to store strong, unique passwords.
